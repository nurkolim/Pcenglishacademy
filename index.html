My Story

Learning English against
all odds

My name is Nur Zaki Kolim — known as Nur Kolim. I am a Rohingya educator originally from Myanmar, currently living in a refugee camp in Bangladesh. I am the son of Mr. Mohammed Shaker ( Matriculation ).

Without access to formal universities or institutions, I built master-level knowledge of the Myanmar English curriculum through nothing but determination, sleepless nights, and an unbreakable passion for education. Every page of these guides was written with struggle, sacrifice, and love for learning.

I believe that knowledge has no borders. No camp, no hardship, and no circumstance can stop a person who truly wants to learn. That is the message behind every guide I create.

To every student reading this — your situation does not define your destination. If Nur Kolim could master English literature from a refugee camp with no resources, you can achieve anything you set your mind to. Keep going. Keep learning. Never give up. 🌟

Every purchase directly supports my mission to prove that education is the most powerful weapon in the world. 💛
